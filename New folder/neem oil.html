NEEM OIL

Target Crops : ALL PLANTS (Cotton, Cabbage, Brinjal, Chilli, Black Gram, Pigonpea)

Target Pest : ALL INSECTS(Boll Worm, Diamond-back Moth, Shoot and Fruit Borer, Pod borer)

Cold Pressed Neem Oil has been used for hundreds of years to control plant insects and diseases.


ADVANTAGES OF NEEM OIL ?

1.Neem oil is nutritious, so it actually acts as a foliar fertilizer.
2.The fatty acids are especially good for plants and some fungi.

DISADVANTAGES OF NEEM OIL ?

1.The use of Cold Pressed Neem Oil because of its low toxicity via all route of exposure.
2. Neem oil can damage plants by burning their foliage.
