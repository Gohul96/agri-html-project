NAME : sulphur

CROPS : onion, garlic, and mustard

ADVANTAGES OF SULPHUR ?

1.Essential for many growth functions in plants including nitrogen metabolism.
2.Enzyme activity and protein and oil synthesis

DISADVANTAGES OF SULPHUR ?


1.High concentrations of sulfur in this setting can cause an increase in salts.
2.Deficiencies can cause serious plant health problems and loss of vitality.
