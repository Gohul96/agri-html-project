NAME: NITROGEN

FORMULA: N -27%

CROPS: Corn,Wheet and Rice

USES:
  • Nitrogen most used by plants for growth and development.
  • Plant needs nitrogen fertilizer to optimize yield.

    • ADVANTAGES OF NITROGEN FERTILIZERS ?

    1.Nitrogen supports vegetative growth. 2.Nitrogen is crucial to plant growth and reproduction.
    3.Pasture and crop growth will often respond to an increased availability of soil nitrogen.

    DISADVANTAGES OF NITROGEN FERTILIZERS ?

    When nitrogen fertilizer is applied faster than plants can use it,
    soil bacteria convert it to nitrate. Water-soluble nitrate is flushed out of soils in runoff,
    where it pollutes groundwater, streams, estuaries, and coastal oceans.
