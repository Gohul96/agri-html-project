NAME : Lambda-Cyhalothrin
INSECTS : all
Lambda-Cyhalothrin has the ability to kill target insects either through coming into physical contact with the chemical or via consumption.
Lambda-Cyhalothrin is a preferred active ingredient for many pest control product manufacturers due to its long-lasting residual effect compared to other chemical ingredients.

lambda-cyhalothrin harmful to humans?


Exposure to Lambda-cyhalothrin poses both acute and chronic risks.
Acute effects include skin and eye irritation, coma, convulsions and severe muscle fasciculation.
