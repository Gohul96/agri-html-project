NAME :PHOSPOROURS

CROPS : Wheat, Barley, Fruits, and Vegetables.

ADVANTAGES OF PHOSPOROURS ?


1.Responsible for assisting with the growth of roots and flowers.
2. Helps plants withstand environmental stress and harsh winters.
3. Improves flower formation and seed production.

DISADVANTAGES OF PHOSPOROURS ?


1.Phosphorus promotes early root growth,seed formation, and increases water use efficiency.
2.Phosphorus can cause increased growth of algae and large aquatic plants,
