NAME: roundup-glyphosate-herbicide

CROPS: ALL WEED PLANTS

ADDVANTAGES OF roundup-glyphosate-herbicide ?

  • control weeds in almost any stage of growth, providing flexibility to the crop growers.
  • reduces the need for labor-intensive weed control.
  • reduce pest pressure and improve crop quality and yield.

    • DISADVANTAGES

    • Glyphosate does not break down rapidly and may leave residue, cause resistance and pollution.
    • Glyphosate can control weeds but being non-selective in nature, it can also effectively kill the main crops.
