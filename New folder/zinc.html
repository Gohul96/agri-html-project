NAME: ZINC SULPHAT

FORMULA: Zn(So)4

CROPS: PADDY CROPS,CORN,BEANS

zinc sulphat is a micronutrient,which is uses along with NPK fertilizers to coserve the zinc content in the soil. The amount of quantity should supply 0.5 to 1.0 lb Zinc per acre when a rate of 20 gallons of water per acre.

USES:

  • To Increase Plant Growth And Development
  • To Improve Soil Fertility

    • HARMFUL TO PLANTS ?

    High concentrations of zinc can cause toxicity in plants. The general symptoms are stunting of shoot, curling and rolling of young leaves, death of leaf tips and chlorosis.

    WHICH PLANTS NEED ZINC ?

    Zinc is a recommended micronutrient in fertilizer programs for production of corn, sweet corn, and edible beans.
