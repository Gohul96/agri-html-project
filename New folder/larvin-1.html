LARVIN

Target Crops : Cotton, Cabbage, Brinjal, Chilli, Black Gram, Pigonpea

Target Pest : Boll Worm, Diamond-back Moth, Shoot and Fruit Borer, Pod borer

ADVANTAGES OF LARVIN ?

1.Larvin is a carbamate insecticide very effective against lepidopteran pests.
2.Larvin is best suited for pest management programmes because it exhibits combined ovicidal, larvicidal, adulticidal and residual activity. 3.Larvin, being a strong stomach poison provides the best solution for the control of gregarious polyphagous leaf feeders like Spodoptera spp.

DISADVANTAGES OF LARVIN ?

1.cause root burn or fertilizer burn.
